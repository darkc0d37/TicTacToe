Support for Tic Tac Toe

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

Thank you for using Tic Tac Toe! If you need help or have any questions, please find the information below.

1. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I play?
A: Tap any empty cell on the grid to place your mark. Players alternate between X and O. The first player to get three in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the round.

Q: How do I start a new game?
A: Tap the "New Game" button below the board to reset the grid and start a fresh round. Scores are preserved between rounds.

Q: Does the app require an internet connection?
A: No. Tic Tac Toe works entirely offline.

Q: Is the app free?
A: Yes. Tic Tac Toe is completely free with no in-app purchases or advertisements.

Q: What devices are supported?
A: Tic Tac Toe supports iPhone and iPad running iOS 16.0 or later.

2. Reporting Issues

If you encounter a bug or unexpected behavior, please contact us with the following details:
- Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15, iPad Air)
- Your iOS version (found in Settings > General > About)
- A brief description of the issue

3. Feature Requests

We welcome suggestions for improving the app. Feel free to reach out with your ideas.
