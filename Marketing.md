App Store Description:

Challenge a friend to the timeless game of Tic Tac Toe — right on your iPhone or iPad!

Take turns placing X's and O's on a sleek, modern game board. Be the first to line up three in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to claim victory. Track your wins with the built-in scoreboard and play round after round to settle the score.

FEATURES:

- Simple, intuitive tap-to-play controls
- Beautiful dark-themed interface with vibrant colors
- Built-in score tracker across multiple rounds
- Winning cells highlighted for instant clarity
- Works completely offline — no internet required
- No ads, no in-app purchases, no data collection
- Lightweight and fast — runs smoothly on any device
- Supports iPhone and iPad

Whether you are killing time on a commute, settling a friendly debate, or introducing a young one to their first strategy game, Tic Tac Toe is the perfect pick-up-and-play companion.
